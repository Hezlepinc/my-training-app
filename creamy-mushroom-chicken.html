Creamy Mushroom Chicken

Restaurant-quality skillet dinner. Bone-in chicken thighs in a thyme-mushroom cream sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 (6oz)
    Bone-in skin-on chicken thighsskin-on is non-negotiable — that's where the fat lives
  • 2 tbsp
    Avocado oilhigh-heat sear; NEVER canola/vegetable
  • 2 tbsp
    Grass-fed butterKerrygold
  • 8 oz
    Cremini or baby bella mushroomssliced
  • 4 cloves
    Garlicminced
  • 1 cup
    Heavy whipping creamfull fat — not "light cream"
  • 1/2 cup
    Chicken brothbone broth preferred — Kettle & Fire or homemade
  • 2 tsp
    Fresh thyme leavesor 1 tsp dried
  • 1/4 cup
    Grated Parmesanblock, freshly grated
  • to taste
    Salt, black pepper

Instructions

  1. Pat chicken thighs completely dry with paper towels. Season generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Heat avocado oil in a large cast iron or stainless skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken skin-side down for 6-7 minutes until skin is deep golden and crisp. Flip and sear 4 more minutes.
  3. Transfer chicken to a plate (it will not be cooked through yet — that's fine). Drain off all but 2 tbsp of fat.
  4. Add butter to the skillet. When melted, add mushrooms in a single layer. Don't stir for 3 minutes — let them brown. Then stir and cook 2 more minutes until deeply colored.
  5. Add garlic and thyme. Cook 30 seconds until fragrant. Pour in chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits.
  6. Reduce heat to medium. Add heavy cream and Parmesan. Whisk until sauce thickens slightly, about 2 minutes.
  7. Return chicken thighs to skillet, skin-side up, nestled into the sauce (don't submerge the skin). Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and cook 8-10 minutes until chicken reaches 165°F internal.
  8. Taste and adjust salt. Serve with cauliflower mash or steamed asparagus.
DeLauer Plan Fit
Meal 1 (noon, post-WOD): 1 thigh + sauce over cauliflower mash = ~750 cal / 7g net carbs. Hits fat target hard, refuels protein after fasted training.
Meal 3 (7pm): 1 thigh + sauce + side salad. Stays under 5g net carbs, keeps insulin low going into the overnight fast.
Why this is clean
Avocado oil + butter for fat, real cream (no thickeners), bone broth, fresh herbs. No "cream of mushroom soup" shortcut (which contains seed oils + modified food starch). Parmesan provides umami without any MSG-containing seasoning blend.
