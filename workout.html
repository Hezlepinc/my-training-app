Progression & Deload

Strength (Tue/Thu): wave the main lift over 3–4 week blocks (5×5 → 4×4 heavier → back-off), then repeat one notch up. Add weight OR reps, never both.

Runs (Wed/Sat): extend distance or add tempo month over month.

WOD / Bodyweight (Fri/Mon): swap module picks each week — fresh stimulus, fixed format.

Deload every 4–6 weeks: one lighter week, ~40% less volume. Joint insurance. Come back stronger.

Why Squat Day & WOD Day Stay Separate

Heavy squat (Tue) is a strength/CNS day — keep it heavy and low-rep. Don't mix it with technical Olympic or overhead work when you're loaded and fatigued.

Overhead squat, snatch, clean, thrusters, CrossFit skill (Fri) go on WOD day as a skill primer when you're fresh, before the metcon. Technical + mobility-heavy = needs a fresh nervous system, not a fried one.

Never before Wednesday's run — that's a recovery/aerobic day; a technical barbell lift muddies its purpose.

Fasted-AM + Keto Notes

All sessions fasted AM. The two hard days (Tue squat, Fri WOD): optional 5g BCAA pre-session in early adaptation; scale intensity ~20% while adapting.

Electrolytes before every session — salt + water, magnesium at night. Same targets as the fast tracker.

Rower note: if shoes slip the stirrups, use flat-soled shoes or socks — or sub bike / run / jump rope.
