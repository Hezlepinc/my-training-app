Chocolate Cheesecake Fat Bombs

Sweet-craving release valve. 1g net carb each. Erythritol/monk fruit only.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz
    Cream cheese, fully softenedfull fat, room temp — Philadelphia or Organic Valley
  • 4 tbsp
    Grass-fed butter, softenedKerrygold
  • 1/4 cup
    Unsweetened cocoa powderDutch-process for darker flavor; regular is fine
  • 1/2 cup
    Powdered erythritol or monk fruit blendMUST be powdered, not granular — granular won't dissolve fully
  • 1/4 cup
    Heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tsp
    Pure vanilla extractnot imitation
  • Pinch
    Sea saltenhances chocolate notes
  • Optional
    Crushed macadamia nuts or cacao nibs for rolling

Instructions

  1. In a stand mixer with paddle attachment (or hand mixer + bowl), beat softened cream cheese and butter on medium speed for 2 minutes until completely smooth — no lumps. This is critical; cold cream cheese leaves chunks.
  2. Add cocoa powder, powdered sweetener, vanilla, salt. Beat on low until incorporated, then medium 1 minute.
  3. With mixer running, pour in heavy cream slowly. Beat 1-2 minutes until mixture lightens slightly and looks like chocolate frosting.
  4. Taste — adjust sweetener if needed. Erythritol can have a cooling effect; if pronounced, a few drops of monk fruit liquid extract can balance it.
  5. Refrigerate the bowl 30 minutes to firm up enough to scoop.
  6. Use a small cookie scoop (1.5 tbsp) to portion 12 balls onto a parchment-lined sheet. Roll between palms to smooth (optional). Roll in crushed macadamias or cacao nibs if using.
  7. Refrigerate at least 90 more minutes before serving. Store in an airtight container in the fridge up to 2 weeks, or freeze up to 6 months.
Meal Plan Fit
Meal 3 (7pm): 1-2 fat bombs after the final meal. Hits dessert-craving without breaking insulin discipline. Keeps you on plan during the hard psychological moment of weeks 1-3 adaptation.
Post-WOD adjacent: If a tough WOD leaves you wanting something cold/sweet immediately, 1 fat bomb is a sane release valve. Better than reaching for "just one bite" of something carb-y.
Cap: 1-2 per day MAX. Fat bombs are dense calories — 250 cal in two bombs adds up if it becomes daily.
Sweetener Discipline
Erythritol or monk fruit ONLY. Do not substitute maltitol (raises blood sugar despite "sugar-free" label, severe GI distress), sucralose, or aspartame. If you buy "powdered sugar-free sweetener" at a store, READ the ingredient list — many blends sneak sucralose or acesulfame K into "monk fruit blends." Lakanto Powdered Monk Fruit and Swerve Confectioners are the clean defaults.
Why this is clean
Real cream cheese (full fat, no thickeners), grass-fed butter, unsweetened cocoa, heavy cream, pure vanilla, approved sweetener. No commercial "sugar-free chocolate" (most contain maltitol). No store-bought "keto fat bombs" — they almost universally contain sucralose, soy protein isolate, or maltitol.
