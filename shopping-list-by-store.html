Shopping Trip Strategy

  • Order Amazon first — Lakanto Powdered Monk Fruit + Kettle & Fire broth. 2-day delivery. Don't shop until they arrive.
  • Costco is the big trip — meats, dairy, eggs, oils, almond flour, vanilla. One Saturday hit covers 2-3 weeks of these recipes.
  • Publix mid-week — fresh herbs, jalapeños, zucchini, coleslaw + the new DIY-condiment items (extra tomato paste, Dijon, anchovy paste, allspice/cloves/cinnamon).
  • Spices & dried herbs — check your spice rack against the spice list. Most carry over from the master grocery list.
  • The "have these already" check — hot sauce, salt, MCT oil, coconut oil, sparkling water — from the master keto list.

DIY Batch Prep (Sunday afternoon, ~30 min, fills your 16 mason jars)

  • Mayo — egg + avocado oil + Dijon + ACV + lemon. Immersion blender. 60 sec. 2 jars.
  • Ketchup — tomato paste + ACV + monk fruit + allspice/cloves/cinnamon. Simmer 10 min. 2 jars.
  • Ranch — mayo + sour cream + heavy cream + ACV + dried dill/parsley/chives + garlic/onion powder. Whisk. 3 min. 2 jars.
  • Caesar — egg yolk + avocado oil + lemon + anchovy paste + garlic + Dijon + Parmesan + pepper. Immersion blender. 5 min. 2 jars.
  • Homemade ranch seasoning (dry) — dill + parsley + chives + garlic/onion powder + salt + pepper. Mix. 2 min. 1 jar.
  • Optional — BBQ sauce, chimichurri, garlic aioli, sugar-free teriyaki. Same kitchen session for a full clean-keto condiment library.
