Bacon Cheeseburger Casserole

Cheeseburger flavor in a 9x13 pan. Family-friendly. Cleaned up from standard recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb
    Grass-fed ground beef, 80/20don't use 93/7 — fat matters
  • 8 slices
    Bacon, choppedPederson's or Applegate — sugar-free, uncured
  • 1/2 medium
    Yellow onion, diced
  • 4 oz
    Cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup
    DIY keto ketchuphomemade in 10 min — see card. Backup: Primal Kitchen Unsweetened. NEVER G Hughes/Heinz "No Sugar" (sucralose).
  • 2 tbsp
    Yellow mustard
  • 1 tbsp
    Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp
    Garlic powder, salt, black pepper (combined)
  • 4 large
    Eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup
    Heavy cream
  • 2 cups
    Shredded sharp cheddarblock, shred fresh
  • Garnish
    Sliced pickles, green onions, shredded lettuce, halved cherry tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Grease a 9x13 baking dish with butter or avocado oil.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook bacon until crisp, ~7 minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon. Drain all but 2 tbsp of bacon fat.
  3. Add diced onion to bacon fat, sauté 3 minutes. Add ground beef and brown thoroughly, breaking up with a wooden spoon, 6-8 minutes. Drain excess fat (leave a couple tablespoons for flavor).
  4. Reduce heat to low. Stir in cream cheese, ketchup, mustard, Worcestershire, garlic powder, salt, pepper. Mix until smooth and beef is fully coated.
  5. Transfer beef mixture to baking dish. Sprinkle 3/4 of the cooked bacon over the top.
  6. In a bowl, whisk eggs and heavy cream. Pour evenly over the beef mixture.
  7. Top with shredded cheddar and remaining bacon.
  8. Bake 25-30 minutes until eggs are set and cheese is bubbly and golden in spots.
  9. Rest 5 minutes. Garnish like a cheeseburger — pickles, green onions, lettuce, tomato. Each diner adds their own.
DeLauer Plan Fit
Meal 1 (noon, post-WOD): 1 serving + side salad = ~800 cal / 6g net carbs. Big-meal anchor that hits all three macro targets in one dish.
Family meal: Non-keto family members can add a bun on the side — the casserole itself is universally appealing. Refrigerates 4 days, freezes well.
Clean-keto swaps applied
Standard "sugar-free" ketchup → DIY keto ketchup (or Primal Kitchen Unsweetened as backup). G Hughes, Heinz No Sugar Added, and similar use sucralose + acesulfame K — disqualifies them. The DIY version is 10 minutes of simmering tomato paste + ACV + monk fruit + spices. Also swapped: standard bacon → sugar-free uncured, pre-shredded cheese → block cheese shredded fresh, "Velveeta-style" creamy base → real cream cheese + heavy cream.
