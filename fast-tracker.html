⚠ 5-Hour Energy — see note
5-Hour Energy note: ~4 cal, won't break the fast metabolically. BUT it's sweetened with sucralose — the sweetener you reacted to this morning — and can trigger a small insulin response. Black coffee does the same caffeine job clean. If you have it, watch your stomach and treat it as the one "dirty" input of the fast.

🔴 Stop the Fast NOW If…

  • Dizziness that doesn't resolve after salt water + sitting
  • Extreme weakness / graying out when standing
  • Sharp headache electrolytes don't fix in 20 min
  • Heart palpitations or irregular beat
  • Confusion or disorientation
Normal — push through: hunger waves (pass in minutes), mild headache (electrolytes fix it), boredom, mildly cold hands. No ego — break early if a red signal hits. Breaking the fast is never failure.

Breaking the Fast (matters as much as the fast)

1
1 cup bone broth first.
WAIT 20–30 MIN
2
Something small & gentle — 2–3 boiled eggs, a few oz salmon, or ½ avocado.
WAIT 1 HOUR
3
Then a normal keto meal. Never break a long fast with a big fatty meal — cramps and nausea.

No Training

Walking only — actually helps. No WOD, no lifting, no metcon during an extended fast in adaptation phase. Ease back in the day after you break.
