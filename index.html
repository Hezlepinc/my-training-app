Plan Your Week

Use the + / − stepper next to each recipe to set how many times you'll make it (e.g. pancakes ×4). The bar at the bottom counts your meals toward your weekly target (default 21 = 3/day — tap the number to change it) and totals the cost. Hit "Build →" for the shopping list, scaled to your quantities. View current list →
