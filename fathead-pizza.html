Fathead Pizza

The mozzarella-and-almond-flour crust that converts skeptics. 4g net carbs per slice.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups
    Shredded mozzarellablock cheese, shred yourself — pre-shredded has cellulose
  • 2 tbsp
    Cream cheesefull fat, room temp
  • 3/4 cup
    Almond flourblanched, fine grind
  • 1 large
    Egg, beaten
  • 1/2 tsp
    Italian seasoning, salt, garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup
    Marinara sauceRao's Sensitive Marinara (no sugar) or Primal Kitchen — check for ≤4g carbs/serving
  • 1 cup
    Shredded mozzarella (topping)
  • 16 slices
    Pepperoni or other clean toppingsApplegate uncured pepperoni — most supermarket pepperoni has dextrose

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine 1.5 cups mozzarella and cream cheese. Microwave 60 seconds, stir, microwave 30 more seconds until fully melted and combined.
  3. Add almond flour, beaten egg, and seasonings. Mix until a uniform dough forms — knead briefly with hands if needed (oil hands if sticky).
  4. Press dough between two sheets of parchment paper into a 12-inch round or rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick. Remove top parchment.
  5. Bake crust alone for 12-14 minutes until golden brown. Poke any large bubbles with a fork.
  6. Remove crust, spread marinara, top with remaining mozzarella and pepperoni. Return to oven for 8-10 minutes until cheese is bubbly and edges brown.
  7. Let rest 3 minutes before slicing into 8 pieces.
Meal Plan Fit
Meal 1 (noon, post-WOD): 3 slices = ~645 cal / 12g net carbs. Pair with side salad (arugula, EVOO, lemon) for fiber and electrolytes.
Meal 3 (7pm): 2 slices = ~430 cal / 8g net carbs. Lighter portion for final meal of the day.
Why this is clean
Real mozzarella (block, shredded fresh), almond flour, marinara with no added sugar, clean pepperoni. No seed oils, no maltodextrin, no preservative-heavy crusts. The "fathead" name comes from the high-fat ratio of the dough.
