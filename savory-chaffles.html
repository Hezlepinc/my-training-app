Savory Chaffles

Cheese + egg waffles. Bread replacement, sandwich vehicle, taco shell. 3g net carbs.

Equipment

Mini waffle maker (Dash Mini, ~$15) — makes 4-inch chaffles ideal for sandwich-bread use. A full-size waffle iron works for larger pieces.

Ingredients (Base Recipe)

  • 1 large
    Eggpasture-raised
  • 1/2 cup
    Shredded mozzarellablock, shred fresh — pre-shredded contains cellulose
  • 1 tbsp
    Almond flour (optional, for crispiness)
  • 1/4 tsp
    Garlic powder
  • Pinch
    Salt

Variations

  • Bacon Cheddar
    Sub cheddar for mozzarella + 1 slice crumbled bacon. Excellent breakfast vehicle.
  • Pizza
    Add 1 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tbsp tomato paste mixed in. Top with marinara + extra cheese, broil 1 min.
  • Taco Shell
    Base recipe; while warm, drape over a wooden spoon handle to form a shell shape. Fill with taco meat, cheese, sour cream.
  • Everything Bagel
    Add 1 tsp Everything Bagel seasoning before cooking. Trader Joe's blend is clean; verify no sugar in others.

Instructions

  1. Preheat mini waffle maker. The Dash takes about 3 minutes to come to temp.
  2. Whisk egg in a small bowl. Add mozzarella, almond flour, garlic powder, salt. Mix to combine.
  3. Spoon HALF the mixture into the waffle maker. Close lid. Cook 3-4 minutes — go by golden-brown color, not the indicator light.
  4. Carefully remove (use a fork at the edge). Repeat with remaining batter.
  5. Let chaffles cool on a wire rack for 90 seconds — they crisp up significantly as they cool. Don't stack them while hot or they go soggy.
  6. Use as sandwich bread, taco shell, pizza base, or pile with butter and eat plain.
Meal Plan Fit
Versatile bread sub — fits any meal slot. Use 2 chaffles as a sandwich for bacon/egg/cheese, BLT with avocado, or burger bun. Pre-make a batch of 8 on Sunday, freeze between parchment, reheat in toaster.
Meal 2 (4pm snack): 1 chaffle + butter + sliced cheese = quick fat hit during the eating window without breaking insulin discipline.
Why this is clean
Two real ingredients (egg + cheese) + optional spices. No commercial "keto bread mix" (most contain modified wheat starch + soybean oil + sweeteners). Block mozzarella shredded fresh — pre-shredded bagged cheese is coated with cellulose and potato starch as anti-caking agents.
